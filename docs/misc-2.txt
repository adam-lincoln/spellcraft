Game modes:
FFA Modes:
Battle - Skirmish in a small arena, the first one to score a set amount of kills wins
Survivor - Send waves of monsters to enemy players while defending yourself from theirs
Hunter - Gather orbs in the forest and hunt your enemies
Conquest - Capture control points which spawn orbs and conquer the labirynth
Team vs Team Modes:
Line Wars - Send waves of monsters to enemy team while defending yourselves from theirs
Co-op Modes:
Gladiator - Team up and fight against waves of monsters with limited pool of orbs
